# How to cite

Any use of TWISTED must cite the following two papers:

1. Tripathi, S., Brahmachari, S., Onuchic, J. N., & Levine, H. (2021). *DNA supercoiling-mediated collective behavior of co-transcribing RNA polymerases.* Nucleic Acids Research, 50(3), 1269–1279. DOI: [10.1093/nar/gkab1252](https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkab1252)

2. Brahmachari, S., Tripathi, S., Onuchic, J. N., & Levine, H. (2024). *Nucleosomes play a dual role in regulating transcription dynamics.* Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 121(28). DOI: [10.1073/pnas.2319772121](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2319772121)
